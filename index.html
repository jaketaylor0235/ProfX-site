FAQ

Questions contractors ask before they start.

Is ProfX a CRM?

No. ProfX is a revenue capture and analytics layer built for home service businesses. It helps you recover jobs, automate follow-up, and understand why quotes are not converting.

Who is this built for first?

ProfX is built for home service businesses, with early focus on roofing and other estimate-driven service companies like HVAC, plumbing, and remodeling.

What kind of analytics does it focus on?

ProfX is designed to show what services clients are turning down, where quotes are being lost, which follow-up sequences perform best, and where missed revenue is hiding.

Do I need to replace my current system?

No. The goal is to work alongside the systems you already use and become the layer that helps you close more jobs and understand what is hurting conversion.
